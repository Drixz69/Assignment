Welcome to Peter Peabody's Driving School

About Peter

Welcome! I am Peter Peabody, a fully qualified Approved Driving Instructor (ADI) based in Basingstoke. I previously worked for a large national driving school, gaining years of experience teaching pupils of all ages, from 17-year-old beginners to mature learners.

I have now gone freelance to offer a more personal, flexible, and affordable service to my local community. My goal is not just to help you pass your test, but to make you a safe driver for life.

The Car & Facilities

You will be learning in a modern, manual transmission vehicle that is fully insured for learner drivers. For your safety and peace of mind, the car is equipped with:

  • He-Man Dual Controls: Ensuring I can intervene safely if necessary.
  • Air Conditioning: Keeping you cool and focused during lessons.
  • Dash Cams: For insurance and reviewing driving scenarios.
